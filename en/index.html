A Space for Pioneering Scientific Ideas

Between rigorous science and science fiction

wAIld Science designates pioneering scientific work that stands between strictly academic science and science fiction. A space for independent researchers exploring bold hypotheses without institutional backing.

Learn More

Note: wAIld Science is a framework for independent scientific research with AI support. Not affiliated with WILD! Science educational products.

What is wAIld Science?

The ZOO Metaphor

A zoological or botanical garden for diverse, self-crafted, previously unseen species of knowledge that most people cannot imagine how they function or what they're for.

Why It Emerged

AI tools enable individuals to realize projects that previously required multi-member teams. Publishing platforms (Figshare, Zenodo, arXiv) provide DOIs without institutional affiliation.

Who Is It For?

For researchers with original concepts whose articles receive ambivalent reviews: "inspiring, interdisciplinarily stimulating" versus "too speculative for publication".

How to Participate

Inclusion in the Catalog

  • Only the author of the work may request inclusion (will be verified)
  • Send your request to info@waildscience.org with:
    • Link to the work (DOI or URL)
    • Brief description of content and contribution (2-3 sentences)
  • An AI assistant is used for initial evaluation, final decision is made by the concept author
  • Inclusion confirms alignment with wAIld Science criteria

Logo Usage

The logo may be used to designate works meeting wAIld Science criteria. Inclusion in the catalog at waildscience.org confirms that the work meets these criteria.
